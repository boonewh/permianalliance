Emergency Shower and Eyewash Trailer

About Our Emergency Shower Trailers

Our emergency shower and eyewash trailers are designed to provide immediate decontamination for workers in the field. These trailers are essential for ensuring safety in hazardous environments and comply with all industry regulations.

Features

  • Fully equipped with emergency shower and eyewash station
  • No generator needed to start
  • 30 PSI on-demand
  • 350 gallon water tank
  • Temperature-controlled water supply
  • Durable and weather-resistant construction
  • We will transport and set up on site
  • Compliance with ANSI Z358.1-2014 standards
