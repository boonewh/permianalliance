Applications

Thaw Frozen Equipment

Winter freeze-ups hit fast. Crews rely on these heaters to quickly thaw valves and manifolds, flowlines and hoses, pumps and fittings, and rig equipment. Targeted heat with tents or ducting gets systems back online quickly.

Support Drilling & Frac Operations

On active wells and frac pads, these heaters prevent hydration units from freezing, blenders and pump trucks from icing, sand equipment lock-ups, and iron and manifolds from stiffening in cold weather. When every stage counts, reliable heat keeps the job moving.

Production Support & Flow Assurance

Cold temperatures create hydrates, reduce flow rates, and cause freeze-offs. These heaters help protect wellheads, separators and treaters, flowback tanks, and water transfer lines. Consistent heat prevents shutdowns and production loss.

Worker Safety & Temporary Facilities

Indirect heat is clean, safe, and ideal for crew trailers, safety tents, control rooms, on-site offices, and break areas. Maintains safe working conditions during extreme weather events.

Construction & Maintenance

These heaters also support pad construction and maintenance tasks including concrete curing, coating and paint work, adhesive and epoxy setup, and equipment repairs under heat tents. They provide stable temperatures when winter would otherwise shut projects down.
